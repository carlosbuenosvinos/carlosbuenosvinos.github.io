Subscribe yourself to PHP internals

By Carlos Buenosvinos

February 2, 2015

On January, 31st, I was in the Barcelona PHP Day. Albert Casademont (@acasademont) was giving his talk about PHP 7. He talked about features, benchmarks and internals. He said that he was subscribed to the PHP Internals mailing list and I thought that more developers should too. However, it’s not so easy to get there.

  1. Visit http://php.net/mailing-lists.php
    1. Look for the “Internals”
    2. Check the Normal (it’s funier)
    3. Scroll down to the input field, write your email and click “Subscribe”
    4. Wait for the confirmation email
    5. Reply and send (no extra content)
    6. Wait for the a second confirmation email
    7. Reply with just the code sent in the second confirmation email
    8. Have fun!
